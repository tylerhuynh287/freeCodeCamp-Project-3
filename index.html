Common Bananas

Listed are 3 of the most common bananas.

Cavendish Bananas

These are the typical bananas found sold in grocery stores, they're bright yellow and somewhat sweet.

Lady Finger Banana

Similar to Cavendish bananas but much shorter and with a sweeter taste.

Plaintain

These bananas are usually used in cooking like fried bananas.
